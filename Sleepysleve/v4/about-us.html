About Us

Sleepysleve Founded in November 2023, Sleepysleve is the brainchild of an ambitious 18-year-old, Vishva. With a passion for fashion and an unwavering commitment to quality, Vishva embarked on a journey to create a brand that stands for comfort, style, and inclusivity. Sleepysleve is not just a clothing brand; it’s a labor of love, designed to make everyone feel good in what they wear.

Our Story

Starting Sleepysleve was no easy task. At just 18, Vishva faced numerous challenges but remained steadfast in the belief that everyone deserves to feel comfortable and confident in their clothes. This journey has been one of creativity, hard work, and a relentless pursuit of perfection. Every stitch, every fabric choice, and every design has been carefully considered to ensure that our products meet the highest standards.

Our Mission

Our mission at Sleepysleve is simple: to make everyone feel good in what they wear. We believe that fashion should be accessible, enjoyable, and inclusive. We aim to see Sleepysleve products worn by people everywhere, spreading a message of positivity and inclusivity.

Join Us On Our Journey

We invite you to join us on this exciting journey. Whether you’re looking for a cozy hoodie or a stylish t-shirt, Sleepysleve has something for you. Explore our collection, find your perfect fit, and become a part of the Sleepysleve family. Thank you for supporting our dream and making Sleepysleve a part of your wardrobe. Together, we can spread good vibes and make the world a more comfortable place, one garment at a time.
