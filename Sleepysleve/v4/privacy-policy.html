Privacy Policy

At Sleepysleve, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is secure. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website or app.

  • Information We Collect

    We collect the following information from our users:

    • Phone Number
    • Email Address
    • Shipping Address
    • IP Address
    • Name
    • Instagram Usernames
    • Geolocation
    • Device Information

  • Use of Information

    Your information is used to process orders, improve your shopping experience, and communicate with you about your order status. We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties, except as required to fulfill your order and enhance your experience.

  • Third-Party Services

    Our website may use third-party services to enhance your experience. These services may collect information as per their privacy policies. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of any third-party services you use.

  • Data Security

    We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. However, please note that no method of transmission over the Internet is completely secure.

Terms and Conditions

  • Order Confirmation

    All orders are subject to confirmation via a phone call within 24 hours of placing the order. If you do not receive a confirmation call within 24 hours, please contact our support team for assistance.

  • Order Cancellation and Refunds

    Customers can cancel their orders and receive a full refund before or during the confirmation call. Refunds will be processed within 24 hours of cancellation. If you do not receive a confirmation call within 24 hours, and there are issues, a refund will be processed within 48 hours of contacting support.

  • Shipping and Delivery

    Orders are delivered within 6 to 7 business days. A delivery fee is applied to each order. Shipping and tracking information will be provided via email or phone number by our delivery team.

  • Payment Methods

    We currently accept only UPI payments. We do not offer cash on delivery.

  • Acceptance of Terms

    By using our website or app, you agree to these terms and conditions. If you have any questions or concerns, please contact our support team for assistance.
